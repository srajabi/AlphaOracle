**Overall Regime Context: Bull Quiet**
This regime generally favors upward trends and breakout continuations, but also offers opportunities for mean-reversion plays on temporary dips or extended runs. Volatility contraction squeezes are especially potent in quiet regimes, as they often precede breakouts in the prevailing trend direction.
