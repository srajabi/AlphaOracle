**Taiwan/China:** The Trump‑Xi ceasefire is “holding – just.” Taiwan tensions remain a tail risk for semiconductors (TSM). A blockade would be a $10 trillion risk. Near term, the ceasefire supports the status quo, but the risk premium on Taiwan‑linked assets (TSM, NVDA supply chain) is underpriced.

**Signal:** **Risk‑off tilt via energy long, consumer discretionary short.** The second‑order inflation shock is the dominant channel.
