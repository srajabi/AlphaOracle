## Portfolio Manager Analysis & Decision

**Key Debates Resolved:**
1.  **Risk vs. Technical Views:** The Risk Managers (both models) urge extreme caution, noting broad breakdowns. The Technical Analyst (deepseek) identifies oversold bounce opportunities in MSFT/AAPL and momentum in NBIS/PLTR. I side with the Risk Managers' macro-prudence but will allocate *tactically* to the strongest technical setups that align with our sector thesis.
2.  **To Hedge or Not:** With 100% cash, direct put options on SPY/QQQ are a cost and complexity. I prefer using cash as the ultimate hedge and deploying small, targeted positions into areas of relative strength or high-conviction mean reversion.
3.  **Sector Priority:** The Macro Strategists are unanimous: Energy (XLE) is the clear leader, and Gold (GLD) is a strategic hedge. Our thesis supports both. Technology exposure should be highly selective, favoring "shovel sellers" (AI infrastructure) and quality oversold bounces.

**Final Strategy:** Deploy **30% of capital** in a disciplined, phased manner. Prioritize defensive/commodity hedges (XLE, GLD) and high-conviction technical setups in resilient sectors. Maintain 70% cash to preserve dry powder for a better entry point, likely after a VIX spike >32 or a sustained SPY recovery above its 20-day SMA.
