5. Regime Change or Rotation?

This is a sector rotation within an existing bullish regime — not a regime change. The bull quiet backdrop remains intact, but the catalyst (Iran deal) forces a re‑pricing of inflation expectations and risk premium.

  • The medium‑term risks (fiscal deficit, high rates, Taiwan flashpoints) have not disappeared — they are simply overshadowed.
  • Positioning adjustment: Reduce energy and gold, increase tech/consumer cyclicals and international, maintain a small tail‑risk hedge.
