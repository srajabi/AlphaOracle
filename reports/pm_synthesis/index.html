Portfolio Manager’s Analysis & Action Plan

The confluence of a hawkish Fed pivot and immediate geopolitical risk (Trump’s April 7 deadline for Iran) creates a high-probability “stagflation-lite” scenario. While we are in a “Bear Quiet” regime, the risk of a violent transition to “Bear Panic” is elevated in the next 24-48 hours.

Our 100% cash position is a strategic advantage. The primary objective is capital preservation. We will deploy capital defensively, focusing on assets that benefit from the prevailing macro drivers (higher rates, inflationary energy shocks, safe-haven demand) or exhibit strong relative strength and low correlation to the vulnerable broad market.

Key Decisions & Rationale:

  1. Gold (GLD) is a mandatory hedge. The divergence between bullish central bank/news flow and its depressed price (hurt by a strong dollar) presents an asymmetric opportunity. An escalation in the Middle East will trigger a catch-up rally.
